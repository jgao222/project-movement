Summary

Project Movement is a speed-focused 2D platformer with a futuristic/abstract pixel art visual style. Across 8+ levels, strive to achieve the fastest time possible by skillfully and precisely combining movement abilities. Complex movement options emerge from the basic ability set, and each level challenges the player to move in a unique way.

History

Project Movement has been in development for 2+ months as part of the CSE 481D Games Capstone course at UW.
